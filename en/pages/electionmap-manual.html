üìç 02. Interactive Voter Map

The Interactive Voter Map is the centerpiece of ElectionMap AI. It provides a high-resolution, zoomable map of your election district with multiple data layers that can be toggled on and off to visualize critical campaign information.

Map Navigation Controls

  • Zoom In/Out: Use scroll wheel or +/- buttons to zoom between national, provincial, district, and precinct levels
  • Pan & Drag: Click and drag to navigate across the map, or use arrow keys for precise movement
  • District Selection: Click on any district to view detailed voter data, historical results, and AI predictions
  • Full Screen Mode: Press F11 or click the expand icon for immersive full-screen map analysis

Data Layers

  • Voter Density Heatmap: Color-coded overlay showing voter concentration by area - darker shading indicates higher voter density
  • Political Affiliation Map: Historical voting patterns displayed with party colors for quick visual analysis
  • Polling Station Locations: Pins showing all polling stations with capacity, accessibility, and expected traffic data
  • Swing District Highlight: AI-identified competitive districts highlighted with special markers and win probability scores
  • Population Flow: Animated overlays showing daily population movement patterns for optimal campaign timing

üí° TIP: Use the layer toggle panel on the right side of the map to enable multiple data layers simultaneously. Combining voter density with political affiliation reveals key strategic targets.

üìä 03. Regional Analysis Dashboard

The Regional Analysis Dashboard breaks down your election district into detailed micro-regions, providing comprehensive data about each area to help you tailor your campaign message and resource allocation.

Dashboard Components

  • Region Summary Cards: Quick-view cards for each sub-region showing population, voter count, turnout history, and partisan lean
  • Comparative Charts: Side-by-side comparison of multiple regions with bar charts, pie charts, and trend lines
  • Issue Heatmap: Color-coded matrix showing which issues matter most in each sub-region based on social media and survey data
  • Resource Allocation View: Visualizes current campaign resource distribution and suggests optimizations

How to Use Regional Analysis

Select District

View Sub-regions

Compare Data

4
Apply Insights

Use the AI recommendations to optimize your regional campaign strategy

üí° TIP: The Region Score feature assigns a 0-100 campaign priority score to each sub-region. Focus your canvassing efforts on regions scoring above 70 for maximum impact.

‚ö° 04. Real-time Election Data

ElectionMap AI continuously ingests and processes election-related data from multiple sources, providing your campaign with the most up-to-date information available. Data refreshes automatically every 15 minutes during active campaign periods.

Data Sources

  • Official Election Data: Voter registration numbers, polling station assignments, and election schedule updates from the National Election Commission
  • News & Media Monitoring: Real-time tracking of election-related news articles, broadcasts, and editorial coverage across all major outlets
  • Social Media Pulse: Aggregated sentiment and trending topic analysis from major social platforms including Twitter/X, Facebook, YouTube, and Korean portals
  • Survey & Polling Data: Integration with authorized polling organizations for the latest approval ratings and issue-based surveys
  • Government Statistics: Census data, economic indicators, and regional development metrics that influence voter behavior

Real-time Alerts

Configure custom alerts to be notified when significant changes occur in your district. Alert triggers include:

  • Polling Shift Alert: Triggered when your approval rating changes by more than 2% within 24 hours
  • News Spike Alert: Notification when your name or key issues are mentioned more than 50 times in an hour
  • Competitor Action Alert: Tracks competitor announcements, events, and policy releases in real-time
  • Voter Registration Alert: Monitors changes in voter registration numbers that could impact your district

‚ö†Ô∏è Note: Real-time data may have a processing delay of up to 15 minutes. For critical campaign decisions, always cross-reference with multiple data sources. Survey data reflects the latest available results from authorized polling organizations.

üë• 05. Voter Demographic Insights

Understand your electorate with deep demographic analysis. ElectionMap AI segments voters into 30+ categories based on age, gender, occupation, education, income level, and residential patterns to help you craft targeted messages.

Demographic Categories

  • Age Distribution: Detailed voter count and turnout probability broken down by 5-year age groups with visual population pyramids
  • Gender Analysis: Male/female voter ratios by region with historical voting pattern differences
  • Occupation Clusters: Major employment sectors in each region - helps identify economic concerns and policy priorities
  • Education Levels: Educational attainment distribution correlated with political engagement and issue sensitivity
  • Residential Patterns: Apartment complexes, single-family homes, rural areas - each with unique outreach strategies

üöó 06. Campaign Route Optimizer

The Campaign Route Optimizer uses AI to design the most effective campaign routes for candidate visits, campaign vehicles, and canvassing teams. It maximizes voter exposure while minimizing travel time and fuel costs.

Route Planning Features

  • AI-Optimized Routing: Algorithms analyze foot traffic, voter density, and time-of-day patterns to create optimal campaign vehicle routes
  • Multi-Stop Planner: Plan candidate visit schedules across multiple locations with travel time estimates and optimal visit duration
  • Canvassing Zone Manager: Divide your district into canvassing zones and assign teams with balanced workloads and priority areas
  • GPS Tracking Integration: Real-time GPS tracking of campaign vehicles with automatic area detection and speech content switching
  • Weather-Adjusted Scheduling: Factors in weather forecasts to recommend indoor vs. outdoor event adjustments

How to Create a Campaign Route

1

2
AI Optimization

AI generates 3 route options ranked by voter exposure potential

3
Review & Edit

Preview routes on the map, adjust stops, and finalize the plan

4
Execute & Track

Launch the route on mobile devices with turn-by-turn navigation and tracking

üí° TIP: Enable the 'Peak Hour Boost' feature to automatically route campaign vehicles through high-traffic areas during rush hours (7-9 AM, 5-7 PM).

üìà 07. Polling & Sentiment Analysis

Track public opinion in real-time with ElectionMap AI's integrated polling and sentiment analysis tools. Monitor how your campaign resonates with voters and identify emerging issues before they become headlines.

Polling Data Integration

  • Multi-Source Aggregation: Aggregates polling data from multiple authorized survey organizations with methodology-weighted averaging
  • Trend Visualization: Interactive time-series charts showing approval rating trends with customizable date ranges
  • Cross-Tab Analysis: Break down poll results by demographics (age, gender, region) to identify strengths and weaknesses
  • Margin of Error Display: All poll visualizations include confidence intervals for accurate interpretation

AI Sentiment Engine

  • Social Media Sentiment: NLP-powered analysis of social media posts categorized as positive, negative, or neutral with topic tagging
  • News Tone Analysis: Automated analysis of news article tone and framing related to your candidacy
  • Comment Mining: Extracts and categorizes voter opinions from online forums, blog comments, and community boards
  • Sentiment Maps: Geographic visualization of public sentiment across your district, overlaid on the election map

üí° TIP: Set up daily sentiment digest emails to receive a morning briefing on overnight sentiment changes, top trending topics, and AI-recommended response strategies.

ü§ñ 08. AI Strategy Recommendations

ElectionMap AI's machine learning engine analyzes all available data to generate actionable campaign strategy recommendations. The AI continuously learns from your campaign's performance and adjusts its suggestions in real-time.

Types of AI Recommendations

  • Resource Allocation: Suggests optimal budget distribution across regions, channels, and time periods based on ROI predictions
  • Message Targeting: Recommends which messages to deliver to which voter segments through which channels for maximum impact
  • Event Planning: Identifies the best locations, times, and formats for campaign events based on expected attendance and media coverage potential
  • Competitive Intelligence: Analyzes competitor movements and suggests proactive counter-strategies
  • Turnout Prediction: Forecasts voter turnout by region and recommends get-out-the-vote (GOTV) focus areas

AI Confidence Scoring

Each recommendation comes with a confidence score (0-100%) based on data quality and historical accuracy. Recommendations are color-coded for easy prioritization:

  • High Confidence (80-100%): Strong data support - recommended for immediate implementation
  • Medium Confidence (50-79%): Moderate data support - review and consider implementing
  • Low Confidence (below 50%): Limited data - use as a reference point, gather more data before acting

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: AI recommendations are strategic suggestions based on data analysis, not guarantees. Always combine AI insights with your campaign team's expertise and local knowledge for the best results.

‚ùì 10. Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What election types does ElectionMap AI support?
+

ElectionMap AI supports all types of public elections in South Korea, including local council, metropolitan assembly, mayoral, gubernatorial, national assembly, and presidential elections. The platform's data layers and analysis tools are pre-configured for each election type with the appropriate geographic boundaries and demographic data.

Q. How accurate is the AI prediction model?
+

Our AI prediction models have achieved over 85% accuracy in recent elections when tested against actual results. However, predictions are estimates based on historical data and current trends. Accuracy improves as more data becomes available closer to election day. We recommend using predictions as one input among many in your strategic planning.

Q. Can I use ElectionMap AI on my mobile device?
+

Yes, ElectionMap AI is fully responsive and works on tablets and smartphones. The mobile interface is optimized for on-the-go use with simplified navigation and touch-friendly map controls. For the best experience with detailed map analysis, we recommend using a tablet or desktop/laptop computer.

Q. How often is the data updated?
+

Data update frequency varies by source: social media and news data refreshes every 15 minutes, polling data updates as new surveys are published, demographic data updates monthly, and official election data updates as released by the National Election Commission. Real-time alerts notify you of significant changes immediately.

Q. Is my campaign data secure?
+

Absolutely. All data is encrypted with AES-256 encryption both in transit and at rest. ElectionMap AI is hosted on certified domestic cloud servers with SOC 2 compliance. Each campaign's data is completely isolated, and we follow strict data privacy policies. All campaign data can be permanently deleted upon request after the election.

Q. Can multiple team members access ElectionMap AI simultaneously?
+

Yes, ElectionMap AI supports multi-user access with role-based permissions. You can create accounts for campaign managers, field coordinators, data analysts, and communication teams with different access levels. Changes made by any team member are synced in real-time across all connected devices.
