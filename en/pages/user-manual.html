üéØ 04. Voter Data Analysis

Comprehensively analyzes SNS, news, and community data to classify voters into 30+ segments and develop hyper-personalized strategies for each group.

Analysis Areas

  • Demographics Analysis: Analyzes voter distribution and voting tendencies by age, gender, occupation, and region
  • Issue Sensitivity Analysis: Scores and prioritizes local issues by interest level and sensitivity

‚öñÔ∏è 09. Election Law Compliance Guide

All features of the AI Election Solution comply with Korean election laws. Please review the key compliance points below.

Key Compliance Points

  • SMS Regulations: Text messages can be sent without limits during the election period, but sender information must be clearly stated
  • Internet Advertising: Support/opposition can be expressed on internet homepages and forums after real-name verification
  • AI Content Labeling: Content generated by AI must be labeled as 'AI-generated content'
  • False Information Ban: All facts in AI-generated content must be verified before publication

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Violations of election law may result in penalties. Please review the Election Law Guide page thoroughly and contact the Election Commission for any questions.
